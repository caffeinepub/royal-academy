# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Update Royal Academy website content to Marathi language for a police aspirant training context, and replace all placeholder images with police training-themed visuals.

**Planned changes:**
- Update all backend hardcoded data (academy name, tagline, description, contact info, course/program titles and descriptions) to Marathi, reflecting police aspirant training topics (MPSC, physical training, law, general knowledge)
- Update all frontend UI text (navigation labels, section headings, button labels, hero text, footer, placeholders) to Marathi so no English strings remain visible
- Replace hero banner, gallery images, and academy logo with new police training-themed images

**User-visible outcome:** The entire Royal Academy website is displayed in Marathi with relevant police aspirant training content, and all images depict academy training activities, uniformed students, and the academy emblem.
